How to Play

AI Ethics Quest teaches Explainable AI and Fairness in Machine Learning through four game modes. Answer questions, complete interactive challenges, and level up your knowledge!

🏰 Story Adventure

Play through 5 chapters as an AI auditor investigating real-world scenarios. Each chapter mixes multiple-choice questions with hands-on interactive challenges:

  • Decision Tree Tracing — Click nodes to follow a patient's path through a decision tree.
  • SHAP Force Plot Builder — Drag features onto a force plot to see how they push a prediction up or down.
  • LIME Word Highlighter — Click words in a message that LIME identifies as spam indicators.
  • Feedback Loop Breaker — Find and cut the right connection to break a bias-amplifying feedback loop.
  • Spot the Bias — Click cells in a dataset that show evidence of unfair outcomes.

Build trust with correct answers. Your trust meter rises and falls based on your choices.

⚡ Quiz Blitz

Race through 20 questions before the 90-second timer runs out. Includes multiple question types:

  • Multiple Choice — Classic pick-the-best-answer questions.
  • Slider — Set a numeric threshold (e.g., α-bias) by dragging a slider.
  • Ordering — Drag items into the correct rank order.
  • Rapid Tap — Tap floating bubbles that match the prompt before time runs out.

Correct answers add +3 seconds to the clock. Build combos by answering correctly in a row — each combo level multiplies your score!

🔬 Scenario Lab

Work through interactive case studies with visuals like decision tree diagrams, SHAP value charts, and α-bias calculations. Also includes:

  • Matching Challenges — Click a term on the left, then its definition on the right.
  • Fairness Scale — Adjust sliders to explore how group rates affect α-bias, with a tilting balance beam visualization.

🐉 Boss Battle

Face two bosses: The Bias Dragon and The Black Box Sphinx. The order you face them and the attacks they use are randomized each time. Answer correctly to deal damage; answer wrong and you take a hit.

Some attacks match a boss's weakness — correct answers on these deal 1.5x critical damage!

⭐ Progression & Power-ups

Your progress is saved between sessions:

  • XP & Levels — Earn XP from every correct answer. Level up from Intern to Legendary Sage (10 levels).
  • Achievements — Unlock badges for milestones like perfect scores, high combos, and defeating bosses.
  • Power-ups — Earn and spend these during gameplay:
    • 50/50 — Removes two wrong answers.
    • Hint — Shows a clue from the explanation.
    • Shield — Blocks the next boss attack (Boss Battle only).
    • Time Freeze — Pauses the quiz timer for 10 seconds (Quiz Blitz only).

Score 80%+ to earn bonus power-ups at the end of each session. Score 90%+ for extra rewards!

⚙ Accessibility

Use the Reduce motion toggle on the main menu to disable all visual effects including screen shake, particle bursts, screen flashes, and CSS animations. Your preference is saved between sessions.

Answer choices and boss encounters are randomized each playthrough, so replaying builds deeper understanding rather than pattern memorization.
